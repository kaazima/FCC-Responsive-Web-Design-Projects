FEATURES

Waterproof

Oral Care electric toothbrush adopts IPX7 waterproof design, which can be directly washed under water, which is convenient, safe & hygienic.

DuPont Bristles

The curved bristles are designed to fit the tooth surface, and the longer ends can penetrate into the brushing area of the cavities & clean the teeth in multiple directions.

3 Brushing Schemes

  • Standard mode: It maintains a frequency of 31,000 vibrations per minute, making deep cleaning suitable for everyone.
  • Gentle mode: The brush meets the frequency of 27,000 vibrations per minute, suitable for sensitive gums.
  • Massage mode: It uses 2 different alternating frequencies every minute to make brushing easier.

2-Minute Timer

The brush is integrated with the vibrating feature to remind users to change the cleaning area so that the entire mouth is cleaned evenly & more efficiently. Besides, electric toothbrush will automatically turn off after 2 minutes to create the habit of brushing science.

Wireless Inductive Charging

Put the Oral Care electric toothbrush on the charging base to quickly charge. Once fully charged, it can be used for about 25 days, twice a day.
